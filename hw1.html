Hexed! - A jQuery Game

Your goal is to guess the RGB values of the swatch on the left using the sliders and the swatch on the left.

Use the sliders or the input boxes to adjust the RGB values of the swatch on the right.

Press the "Got it!" button when you think you have the correct answer.

You have a maximum of '10' turns by default.
